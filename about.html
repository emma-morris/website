About me

Hello, I'm Emma - a newly qualified UI/UX designer hoping to further my career in the digital space.

Currently, I'm a designer at Suru Partners, a digital consultancy and software development firm. Here I help organisations take their digital strategy from early ideas to working designs.

Before joining this posititon, I worked as a graphic designer for over 3 years where I specialised in editorial layouts, branding and inclusive design. After taking on more digital based projects I found a love for this area and problem solving so I decided to enroll in a UI immersion course with Career Foundry.

Outside of design, I love spending time with friends and family, going to the theatre, baking or binging my favourite shows on Netflix! I'm always looking for new challenges and new people to collaborate with! Reach out and let's chat!
